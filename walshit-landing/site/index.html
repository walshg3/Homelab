The short version

No ads. No algorithm. Nothing autoplaying something you didn’t ask for.

Your invite gets you into Plex and the tools around it: Framerr to browse the collection, Seerr to ask for something that isn’t in it, Audiobookshelf to listen (that one may want a login of its own). It’s a home setup rather than a company, so things get added, tidied, and occasionally rearranged—but nothing here is trying to keep you watching.

Setup & help

In before the trailers.

There are no public signups here, so nothing will open until your account has been added. Once your invite arrives, this is the whole process.

  1. Accept the inviteOpen the email or message you were sent. It’s tied to you, so don’t pass it on to anyone else.
  2. Sign in to PlexUse the account the invite was sent to, then accept access to the shared library.
  3. Browse, then askFramerr shows what’s on the shelves. If something isn’t there, request it in Seerr.
  4. Bookmark your doorsSave the services you actually use. Audiobookshelf may need a separate login—ask for one.

Stuck? Reply to your invite and say which step you got to and what you’re using—phone, TV, laptop. Never send a password or access code to anyone, including us.
