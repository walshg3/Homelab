Looking for something that isn't already in the library? Request it
through Seerr instead of asking around.

## Where to go

Open [requests.walshit.com](https://requests.walshit.com) and sign in
with the same account you use for Plex.

## What to do

Search for the movie or series, then submit a request. You'll be able to
see its status — pending, approved, or already available — without
needing to follow up.

## What to expect

Requests get reviewed and added when they're available, not on a fixed
schedule. There's no notification system built into this site; check
back on Seerr or the [dashboard](https://home.walshit.com) if you're
curious about status.

Audiobook requests aren't handled through Seerr. If you're missing a
title on [Audiobookshelf](https://books.walshit.com), ask whoever
invited you directly.
