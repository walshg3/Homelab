Access to Walshit is by invitation only. Once your invite arrives, follow these four steps to get started.

## 1. Accept the invite

Open the link or message you were sent. It's tied to you, so don't pass
it on to anyone else. The invite will guide you through creating an account and signing in to the services you were invited to.

## 2. Sign in to Plex

Use the account the invite was sent to, then accept access to the shared
library at [app.plex.tv/desktop](https://app.plex.tv/desktop/).

## 3. Check in, then ask

[dashboard](https://home.walshit.com) is your home base for updates and
shortcuts. If something is missing from the library, request it through
[requests](https://requests.walshit.com) — see the
[requesting media](/guides/requesting-media/) guide for details.

## 4. Bookmark your services

Save the services you actually use.
[books](https://books.walshit.com) may need a separate login —
ask whoever invited you for one.

**Stuck?** Contact whoever invited you. Tell them which step you reached
and whether you're using a phone, TV, or laptop. Never send anyone your
password or access code, including the person running the server.
