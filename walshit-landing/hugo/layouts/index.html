The short version

No ads in the library. No algorithm to please. Nothing designed to keep you watching.

Once you're invited, Plex is where you watch, Framerr is your home base, Seerr handles requests, and Audiobookshelf is where the books live—it may need a login of its own. It's a home setup rather than a company, so things get added, tidied, and occasionally rearranged.
