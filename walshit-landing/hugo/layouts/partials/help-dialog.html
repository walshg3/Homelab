Setup & help

Before the trailers.

Access is by invitation only. Once your invitation arrives, this is the whole process.

  1. Accept the inviteOpen the email or message you were sent. It's tied to you, so don't pass it on to anyone else.
  2. Sign in to PlexUse the account the invite was sent to, then accept access to the shared library.
  3. Check in, then askFramerr is your home base for updates and shortcuts. If something is missing from the library, request it in Seerr.
  4. Bookmark your doorsSave the services you actually use. Audiobookshelf may need a separate login—ask for one.

Stuck? Contact whoever invited you. Tell them which step you reached and whether you're using a phone, TV, or laptop. Never send anyone your password or access code, including the person running the server.
